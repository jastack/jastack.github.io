Lone Ranger Cookies

Description

Lone Ranger cookies are delious and remarkably easy to make. They were originally a staple of cowboys with a sweet tooth, who often found themselves in remote corners of the West with only their cows for company and a craving for home-baked pastries. All the ingredients were something any reasonably competent cowboy could wrangle up from the local trading post.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups oats
  • 3/4 cups coconut
  • 12 oz choc chips

Directions

  1. First, cream together the butter, brown sugar, and white sugar.
  2. Then add the eggs and vanilla.
  3. Mix the flour, soda, and salt together in a separate bowl.
  4. Combine the dry ingrediants with the wet ones.
  5. Add the oats, coconut, and the chocolate chips.
  6. Scope onto a cookie sheet and bake for 10-11 mins at 350 degrees.
  7. Enjoy!
